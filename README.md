**Muse-AI** is a cutting-edge generative framework that explores the intersection of neural networks and musical aesthetics. Designed with a focus on **Cultural Synthesis**, Muse-AI doesn't just generate notes; it maps harmonic structures to cultural parameters, enabling the synthesis of music that resonates with specific regional and emotional contexts.

## 🌟 Key Capabilities

- **🎹 Neural Composition:** Autoregressive melody generation using transformer-based architectures.
- **🌍 Aesthetic Mapping:** Directing generation through cultural scales and regional harmonic logic.
- **🧐 Cultural Critic Engine:** AI-driven analysis of creative outputs for "Aesthetic Resonance".
- **🎼 Multi-Track Synthesis:** Synchronized generation of rhythm, harmony, and lead motifs.
- **🌀 Mood Modulation:** Real-time adjustment of generative parameters based on emotional targets.
